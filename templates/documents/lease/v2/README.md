# lease/v2

This is the standard England assured-periodic tenancy agreement for new
tenancies created on or after 1 May 2026. It is rendered directly by
`src/lib/contract-generation/render.ts` without a browser dependency.

The content follows the written-information headings published in the
government's assured-periodic-tenancy landlord guidance. It intentionally
does not reproduce the full statutory possession grounds, impose a fixed end
date, require a deposit reference before signing, preset an early-termination
charge, or contract out of landlord repair obligations.

It is only suitable after confirming that the letting is an assured periodic
tenancy in England. It is not intended for lodgers, company lets, holiday
lets, business tenancies, qualifying purpose-built student accommodation, or
other excluded arrangements.
